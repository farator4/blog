## About Unicorn

Description: Unicorn comes equipped with many options to tweak the way it shows your content. There are options to enable or disable sidebars, 3 nav menus, favicon, scroll to top button, custom header, logo and background (or have no header and background at all), options to change colors of design elements and the option to enable or disable header image and text on not core pages. You can also choose to display your reñent posts or a custom page to be shown at the site’s front page with it’s own unique widgetized areas. Unicorn has 2 custom widgets for displaying social media icons and slider. It can show up a slideshow at a different frame rate speed in any sidebar. Translation ready. You can find more info and demo at http://wpblogs.ru/themes/blog/theme/unikorn/.

## How to install

* Unzip and then copy the jolene directory to /wp-content/themes/ in your WordPress installation. 
* Activate from admin menu. 

## How to use

* Theme options placed at "Appearance >> Customize"
* Widgets can be found at "Appearance >> Widgets"
* How to setup a slider: http://wpblogs.ru/themes/blog/configure-sliders-for-your-website/
